The foot racing events, which include sprints, middle- and long-distance events, _____ walking and hurdling, are won by the athlete with the fastest time. -- race

Track and field is _____ which includes athletic contests established on the skills of running, jumping, and throwing. -- a sport
The name is derived from the sport's typical venue: a stadium with an oval running track enclosing a grass field where the throwing and _____ of the jumping events take place. -- some
Regular jumping events include long jump, triple jump, high jump and _____, while the most common throwing events are shot put, javelin, discus and hammer. -- pole vault
Track and _____ is categorized under the umbrella sport of athletics, which also includes road running, cross country running, and race walking. -- field
The jumping and throwing events are won by the athlete who achieves the greatest distance or _____. -- height
